About Us

MT Longonot Mabati Factory is an upcoming manufacturer of high quality roofing sheets. We provide reliable and durable roofing solutions to homes and businesses across Kenya. We are located in Mirera Mastima Centre along Naivasha-Maai Mahiu highway in Naivasha.

Why Choose Us

  • Trusted and Reliable. We sell high quality roof sheets which meet the roofing industry standards and are able to withstand every local weather conditions.
  • Affordable Pricing. We offer the best prices in the current market for the different varieties of mabati to meet the needs of our customers.
  • Skilled Professionals
  • Timely Project Completion. We provide transportation services to our customers and timely delivery.
  • Customer Satisfaction Guaranteed
